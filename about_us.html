Yunjie Li

My name is Yunjie Li, a senior student currently studying

Michael Keo

My name is Michael Keo and I'm currently a senior in
computer science at Iowa State University. I have some
experience working in front-end development through my
current internship at State Farm and have done independent
studying on the side. I've created a personal
portfolio webpage prior to taking this course that has
helped me understand how the front-end comes together.

As a huge fan of watching movies, I've always struggled
with finding new movies to watch and so a catalog, where
users are able to easily find new movies has always been
on my mind. Although this idea isn't new with more
established websites like Rotten Tomatoes and IMDB
conquering this field, this sort of webpage is something
that I've always thought of creating.
